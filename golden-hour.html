The Story

Kept on the road, by hand.

Joe & Bobbi · Palm Springs

Coachella Valley Classic Cars is a small operation in the Palm Springs desert with a single, stubborn devotion: keeping these cars alive. Each one in the fleet is restored and maintained by hand so it can keep doing what it was built to do — turn an ordinary afternoon into something people photograph forever.

Joe and Bobbi run it the way regulars describe them — “always a wonderful couple to work with.” Every booking comes with one of them, or a driver who stays with the car the whole time: positioning it, protecting it, moving it as many times as the shot needs. You focus on the picture; the car is handled.

No self-drive, no dealership polish, no upsell. Just well-kept machines, delivered where you need them, across Palm Springs, the Coachella Valley, Joshua Tree, Twentynine Palms and Yucca Valley.
