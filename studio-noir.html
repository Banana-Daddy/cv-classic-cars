The studio

Two people.
One garage.

Joe & Bobbi — Palm Springs

Coachella Valley Classic Cars isn’t a dealership. It’s a small operation run by Joe and Bobbi, who keep a handful of classic cars alive in the Palm Springs desert and send them out to do what they were built for — be seen.

Every car is restored and maintained by hand. Every booking comes with a driver who stays with the vehicle the whole time: placing it, protecting it, repositioning it as many times as the shot demands. Regulars describe them simply — “always a wonderful couple to work with.”

No self-drive. No upsell. Just well-kept machines delivered where you need them, across Palm Springs, the Coachella Valley, Joshua Tree, Twentynine Palms and Yucca Valley.
